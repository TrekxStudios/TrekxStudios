About Me.

Hi, Im Ryan. I'm a faith-driven videographer based out of Pennsylvania. I have a passion for creating high quality imagery that tells stories. From brand promotion, to special events and memories; I combine high quality video with high level editing to meet my clients expectations.

Life can move fast, but it is a long journey: A Trek; which is defined as a long journey (typically taken on foot). Trekx Studios is here to meet you where you are on that journey and simply allow the world to see your story in movie-quality video.
