About Me.

Hi, I’m Ryan — a Christ-driven videographer based in Pennsylvania & SoCal.
At Trekx Studios, I craft cinematic videos that tell your story. Whether it’s a brand, a special event, or a lifelong memory; I'll handle every aspect of creating imagery that resonates with heart and purpose.

Life may move fast, but it's a journey — a trek — and that is the base of Trekx Studios. My mission is simple: to meet you where you are and showcase your story in stunning, movie-quality video.

Retainer Packages

  • Starter Up to a 2 hour shoot day, one 15 minute Zoom for planning, 3 vertical videos, basic editing and light branding.
  • Growth One 3 Hour shoot day, 30-45 minute Zoom planning, 5 vertical videos, advanced editing, text animation & transitions, branded animations.
  • Premium+ Customized pricing. 4 to 5 hours (or 2 days) shooting, Stategic planning and review, Premium editing and graphics, sound design, 3d text elements and custom branding. Priority scheduling.

Single Production

  • Intro (pricing can scale to meet project needs) 1 hour shoot with one edited video. Ideal for small business, one time promos, or personal use.
  • Spotlight Up to 3 hour shoot. 10 edited photos, 2 edited videos.
  • Event Recap Full-day event coverage. 15-20 edited photos, 2 highlight videos of event coverage. Optional drone add-on.

Real Estate

  • Photo Essential Professional HDR images of property interior & exterior (15-20 images) perfect for MLS listing and rentals.
  • Interior Showcase Interior videography walkthrough and 10-15 edited HDR images. Great for highlighting properties.
  • Premium Showcase Full property coverage. Interior + exterior videography with aerial drone video. Up to 25 HDR images.
